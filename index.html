Welcome to the game!

Mafia is a game where a group of players are assigned a variety of roles. Mafia (amount of mafia depends on amount of players in the game), 1 doctor, and 1 detective. The rest of the players are assigned innocent. Every night the mafia vote on who to kill, then the doctor votes on who to save, and then the detective decides on who to investigate.
